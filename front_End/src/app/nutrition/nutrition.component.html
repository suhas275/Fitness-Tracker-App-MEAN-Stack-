BMI (Body Mass Index) based on their weight classification.

  • The user's BMI indicates that they fall into either the overweight, underweight, or normal weight category.
  • If the user's BMI indicates overweight, they may be presented with options such as personalized diet plans, exercise routines, and lifestyle modifications to help them achieve a healthier weight.
  • On the other hand, if the user's BMI indicates underweight, the webpage offers recommendations for increasing calorie intake, strength training exercises, and advice on maintaining a balanced diet to support healthy weight gain.
  • These options empower the user to make informed choices regarding their health and well-being, encouraging them to take steps towards achieving their desired weight and improving their overall quality of life.
